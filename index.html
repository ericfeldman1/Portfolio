About Me

Hey, I'm Eric, and I'm currently studying web development through Bloc's Web Developer program.

I've always been excited about learning new things, and have been doing a lot of that in recent years. Since training as a materials engineer in college, I've been spending my post-college days learning to crack the code of ancient Jewish texts in Jerusalem, teaching science to high school drop-outs in Chicago, and estimating multi-million dollar construction projects in Baltimore.

My newest venture is coding, and with it, I've finally found a niche that I feel confident that I can stay put with for a while. I love the new skills that are allowing me to be a contributor to the tech world that I am unavoidably engaged in on a daily basis. It is extremely gratifying to be acquiring the skills to take ideas and then make them a reality - I'm slowly beginning to see that almost anything is possible. Although frustrating at times, I love the continual process of slowly developing an app and constantly making improvements until I am satisfied with it, as this is how I like to approach all of life.

Aside from coding, I love to spend my time watching The Office with my amazing wife, jumping in puddles with my son, and biking. meditating, and doing yoga when I can find a spare moment.
